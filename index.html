Early Head Start Sight Words

Sight Words are a collection of words that a child should learn and recognize. Learning Sight Words helps build a child vocabulary and build a foundation for reading. It is important to go over these words weekly with your child. Try making flash cards, it help the child learn them faster. A List of Sight Words Are Listed Below:

Weekly Sight Words

  1. I
  2. at
  3. is
  4. to
  5. like
  6. me
  7. it
  8. am
  9. and
  10. a
  11. in
  12. here
  13. up
  14. go
  15. see
  16. my
  17. you
  18. the
  19. we
  20. on
