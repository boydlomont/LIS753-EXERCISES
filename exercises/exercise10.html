How to Teach Sight Words

A child seeing the Sight Word on a flash card and saying it, while underlining the Sight Word with their finger will help them memorize the word.

Tell a story that repeats different sight words, this will help the child indentify the words.

Play word games with letter blocks, mix up the letter and see what word they can come up with. Games like Word Memory and BINGO are fun.

Cards can be made or brought on amazon.com.
