What is Autism?

Autism, or autism spectrum disorder (ASD), refers to a broad range of conditions characterized by challenges with social skills, repetitive behaviors, speech and nonverbal communication. According to the Centers for Disease Control, autism affects an estimated 1 in 59 children in the United States today.

The this group is inspiring community-based social platform for families in Illinois that care for loved ones with Autism. We are committed to building awareness and gaining recognition for supporting and empowering families dealing with the daily challenges of Autism.

"Autism, is part of my child, it's not everything he is. My child is so much more than a diagnosis."
