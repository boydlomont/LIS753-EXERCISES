Used Book Sales

We have over 50,000 wonderful used books, records, videos, magazines, and more at our monthly sale. These are mostly from donations from individuals, estates, and companies. We also sell items that the Palo Alto library has removed from their shelves to make room for new purchases.

    Hours on Saturday's

    Children's and Bargain Rooms- 7:00 am
    Main Room- 8:00 am

    Some customers come very early on Saturday to ensure an early place in line; then go off and get coffee or to visit the Children's and Bargain Book rooms while waiting.

    Rules

    1. A maximum of two numbered tickets per person at the Main Room, beginning at 8 am on the Saturday of the sale.
    2. Because of our severe space limitations and compelling issues of health and safety, we will only allow 160 customers into the Main Room at a time.
    3. On Saturdays, customers will be originally admitted in the order of their numbered tickets that are given out from 8 to 11 am.
    4. Please be sure to be in Main Room a few minutes before it opens to reclaim your spot in the line; miss your time of entry will forfeit your place in line.
    5. Once inside, customers may take only 12 books off of shelves, at a time will continue past noon if a line remains outside.
    6. They should purchase items than exit via the east door.
    7. They may then join at the end of any remaining line at the north door and reenter in that order.
    8. If you don't arrive in time to get a ticket, please just join the latter part of the line.
